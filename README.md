# CommonsLens: High-Performance Discovery Engine for Wikimedia Commons

CommonsLens is a UI layer designed to navigate the Wikimedia Commons media API with maximum efficiency. It provides users with granular filtering, semantic characterization, and optimized viewing of the entire global media archive, made for everything casual discovery, finding the best free-to-use images, or even scientific data-driven workflows.

## Global Media Discovery
Search across one of the largest free media libraries in history, ranging from high-resolution imagery and complex documents to professional-grade audio and video assets.

<img width="1856" height="1051" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6b8879bb-a6f1-49b0-8875-80c580faa4c1" />

*   **High-Speed Retrieval:** The engine is built for persistent query execution, allowing you to search millions of records without experiencing UI latency or system lockups.
*   **Multi-Modal Support:** Query across diverse media types including vector diagrams, photographs, office documents, and raw audio files.
*   **Simplified Navigation:** A familiar search interface ensures that both broad queries and specific technical requests yield precise results immediately.

## Granular Metadata and Semantic Filtering
CommonsLens uses an advanced filter suite to isolate specific assets by file format, licensing requirements, subject depictions, and precise temporal or spatial constraints.

<img width="1856" height="1051" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5821bad2-9164-4ff0-b8fb-b093693a89ab" />

*   **Coordinate Targeting:** Apply coordinate filters to discover media created within specific radius distances of any global location.
*   **Semantic Intelligence:** Use Wikidata Q-codes via the dropdown to filter by specific subjects, objects, or concepts, ensuring that results match your exact research topic.
*   **Deep-Category Exploration:** Utilize category hierarchy tools to narrow your scope effectively, whether you are looking for broad subjects or highly specific taxonomies.

## Key Media Metadata
Each media entry provides a transparent, detailed breakdown of its provenance, licensing, and structural metadata.

<img width="1851" height="1046" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/773e9338-a058-4655-8b03-ca22dd7da9fa" />

*   **Attribution and Provenance:** Instantly view the creator of the asset, original upload timestamps, and associated artist profiles.
*   **Structured Licensing:** Every file displays its specific Creative Commons license, providing clear usage terms for academic, research, or commercial reference.
*   **Technical Specifications:** Access precise data regarding file dimensions, MIME types, and original creation dates to ensure the media meets your project requirements.

## Dataset Compilation
Create and curate your own research datasets with an efficient, client-side export workflow that processes your selections in real time.

<img width="1851" height="1046" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a028fb3e-cee0-4552-a1a4-97bf621be980" />


*   **In-Memory Processing:** Perform bulk downloads without relying on external servers, as all ZIP archives are generated directly within your browser for increased speed and security.
*   **Batch Exporting:** Long-press any media card to initiate selection mode, allowing you to aggregate hundreds of files into a single, downloadable archive.
*   **Persistent Filtering:** Download operations respect your active filter state, ensuring that the exported data matches your research criteria perfectly.
